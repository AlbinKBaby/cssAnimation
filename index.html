Batman

Fictional character

Batman is a superhero who appears in American comic books published by DC Comics. Batman was created by the artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger, and debuted in the 27th issue of the comic book Detective Comics on March 30, 1939.
Creators: Bob Kane, Bill Finger
Children: Damian Wayne, Huntress, Robin
Romantically involved with: Catwoman, Wonder Woman, Vicki Vale
Powers and abilities: Flight, Martial Art
Notable aliases: Bat-Man, Batman, Batman / Bruce Wayne, Batman 2, Batman/Bruce Wayne, Bats, Bruce Wayne
Parents: Thomas Wayne, Martha Wayne
Foes: Joker, Penguin, Harley Quinn, Riddler
